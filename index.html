Our Story

Farm4Glass started with a simple frustration: DECA preparation felt scattered, overwhelming, and inconsistent. As competitors ourselves, we wanted a better way to prepare for exams, roleplays, and written events. Additionally, all three of us enjoy graphic design, and we know it is a hard skill to develop. We made courses on how to use Canva and Adobe platforms specifically for DECA, to level up your marketing skills.

We built Farm4Glass to bring everything together in one place: structured courses, realistic practice, gamified progression, and resources created by students who have actually competed in the past. We have also invited guest speakers, who are previous ICDC Winners and Finalists for their event to share tips and tricks to help all of you in your competitive success.

Our mission is simple: help every DECA student walk into competition day feeling confident and prepared.

Her DECA competitive success includes placing 6th at California DECA's State Career Development Conference 2026 (qualifying to ICDC), receiving finalist for AAM and EFB at the NorCal Career Development Conference, and earning 1st place in AAM at the Tri-Valley Minicon in 2025. At first, Aarushi used to struggle with understanding how to study for her DECA cluster exam, and she hopes students in the same boat find these courses helpful to learn the material in the most efficient way possible.
